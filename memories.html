This is where we'll collect photos, stories, and memories from the WRHS Marching Band years. We're just getting started — help us fill this page.

We need your photos

Got pictures from band camp, football games, competitions, the state championship, bus trips, or just hanging around the band room? We want them all.

Send your photos and stories to marchingfarmers1996@gmail.com and we'll add them here.

Bonus points if you can tell us who's in the photo and when it was taken.

Share a Memory

Don't have a photo? That's fine. Send us a story, a moment, a thing you'll never forget. We'll collect the best ones and share them here (with your permission).
