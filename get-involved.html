Get Involved

We're trying to track down as many 1996-era band members as possible. If you're reading this, you can help.

Share the Word

Send this site to anyone you're still in touch with from WRHS band. The more people who register, the better the reunion will be.

Dig Out Your Photos

Got photos from band camp, competitions, rehearsals, or the state championship? We'd love to collect them. Email them to marchingfarmers1996@gmail.com and we'll add them to the site.

Help Us Find People

If you know someone's married name, current city, or social media handle, pass it along. We don't want anyone to miss this because they're hard to Google.

Help Us Organise

A reunion doesn't plan itself. We're currently looking for help with:

  • Venue — know a good indoor spot in the Wheat Ridge/Denver area that can hold a crowd? We're all ears.
  • Food — catering ideas, restaurant connections, or someone who just really wants to be in charge of the food.
  • Drinks — brewery contacts, bar recommendations, or someone willing to organise the drinks situation.

If you're good at this kind of thing and want to help make it happen, get in touch at marchingfarmers1996@gmail.com
