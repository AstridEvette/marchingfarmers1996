Wheat Ridge High School Marching Band Reunion

In the autumn of 1996, the Wheat Ridge High School Marching Band won the 4A Colorado State Marching Band Championship.

Thirty years on, we're getting the band back together.

Autumn 2026 — Date and Venue TBC

We're planning a reunion for anyone who was part of the WRHS Marching Band in 1996 — or thereabouts. If you were in the band a few years either side, you're one of us. Come along.

Details are still being finalised. Register your interest now and we'll keep you posted as plans take shape.
